# Code of Conduct

*The CrystalClear Skill OS community standard.*

---

## Our Pledge

We are building the operating system for human-aligned intelligence.
That work demands a community that is itself aligned — with honesty, respect, and purpose.

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## Our Standards

### We expect:

**Intellectual honesty**
Build skills that do what they say. Write contributions that say what they mean. Disagree with ideas, scrutinize specs, challenge assumptions — but do it with evidence, not ego.

**Respectful disagreement**
Architecture debates are healthy. Personal attacks are not. Critique the skill, not the person who wrote it.

**Constructive feedback**
Every review is a teaching moment. Be specific. Be kind. Be useful.

**Accountability**
We govern our systems. We govern ourselves. If you make a mistake, own it cleanly and correct it.

**Inclusion**
The best intelligence systems are built by diverse minds. We actively welcome contributors from all backgrounds and experience levels.

---

### We do not accept:

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks of any kind
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Deliberately demoralizing contributors, especially newcomers
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Submitting skills designed to deceive, harm, or manipulate
- Bad-faith governance circumvention
- Spam, self-promotion unrelated to the project

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any community channels associated with Skill OS.

It also applies when an individual is representing the project in public spaces.

---

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a GitHub issue marked `[conduct]` or by contacting the maintainers directly.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated. The response will be proportional to the violation:

1. **Warning** — private written warning, clarification of expected behavior
2. **Temporary suspension** — temporary ban from community spaces
3. **Permanent removal** — permanent ban for repeated or severe violations

Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality regarding reporters.

---

## Our Commitment

We will uphold this standard.
We will enforce it consistently and fairly.
We will evolve it as the community grows.

Because the integrity of this community
is as important as the integrity of the system we're building.
